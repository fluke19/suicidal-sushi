At EJF Search we partner with leading brands to identify exceptional individuals with the appropriate experience, skill sets and culture fit for some of the most demanding roles in the fashion, luxury and lifestyle industries. We have developed a profound knowledge of these industries and have perfected our search procedures through practical experience.

EJF is led by Emma Farrant who has a classically trained background in Executive Search, with over 10 years’ experience working on high profile searches across the retail and consumer brands industry. Emma has worked for a number of highly successful boutique firms, and in 2007 she established her own consultancy which focuses on partnering her business across the breadth of the retail industry.

We develop a professional and trusting relationship with our clients, to do that we keep everything private so you can have a peace of mind.

  • The Process

    Working to the agreed search strategy, we deliver extensive research across the appropriate sectors, geographies and target companies.

  • Understanding our clients

    It is vital that we understand from the outset, the vision, long-term strategies and goals, as well as the culture and dynamics of our clients’ organisations.

  • Research

    We provide our clients with the appropriate tools and insight to elevate their knowledge of the candidate pool and industry function which is being focused on.

  • Search Methodology

    We build trust quickly, and enable individuals to explore opportunities and have curious discussions.

  • Completing the process

    We coach them at each stage, and manage the discussions on remuneration and contracts, as well as supporting their resignation and exit date from their current employer.
