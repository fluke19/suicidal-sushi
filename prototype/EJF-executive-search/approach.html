Our Approach

THE PROCESS

We pride ourselves on delivering an exceptionally well-organised and effective search process. We provide our clients with the appropriate tools and insight to elevate their knowledge of the candidate pool and industry function which is being focused on. Our search methodology is clear, structured and works to an agreed timetable.

We are highly experienced in working on discreet and / or covert search assignments, which require a sensitive approach to protect confidentiality.

UNDERSTANDING OUR CLIENTS

It is vital that we understand from the outset, the vision, long-term strategies and goals, as well as the culture and dynamics of our clients’ organisations. We spend time with our clients at the beginning of the process to gain this valuable, detailed knowledge, which allows us to talk about the businesses we represent with credibility.

RESEARCH

Working to the agreed search strategy, we deliver extensive research across the appropriate sectors, geographies and target companies. We pride ourselves on our research presentation and the knowledge that this provides our clients.

New market research is conducted for all assignments, and all data is qualified.

SEARCH METHODOLOGY

Candidate Experience

All individuals contacted as candidates or sources are approached in a professional and engaging manner. We build trust quickly, and enable individuals to explore opportunities and have curious discussions. We take time to really bring to life the clients and brands that we represent, and we feel that this contributes to elevating their employer brand appropriately in the marketplace.

The Interview Process

From the research conducted, we will interview candidates with the appropriate skills and experience, with a view to evaluate their commercial capability against the role description, as well as assessing their culture fit and long-term potential.

We are also able to provide psychometric assessments, or partner with your preferred assessment provider.

COMPLETING THE PROCESS

Referencing

We personally conduct a minimum of two informal references for the shortlist candidate(s), from a previous Line Manager, or Business Owner / Investor for board-level searches.

Negotiations

We support and stay close to our candidates throughout the entire process. We coach them at each stage, and manage the discussions on remuneration and contracts, as well as supporting their resignation and exit date from their current employer. We provide a balanced view at this stage, which supports both our clients and candidates fairly.

On-Boarding

After the assignment is completed, we remain in close contact with both our client and the candidate.

We strive to set candidates up for success in their new organisation, and work closely with our clients to assist in the on-boarding process.

Once the candidate is in post, we also enjoy regular follow up calls and discussions to hear how they are performing, and feed back where necessary.
